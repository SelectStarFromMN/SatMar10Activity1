How to make a turkey sandwhich:

  1. Get 2 slices of your favorite bread.
  2. Butter one slice of the bread.
  3. Place your desired amount of deli sliced turkey on the buttered bread.
  4. Place a slice of swiss cheese atop the sliced turkey.
  5. Add some lettuce, tomato, onion or other garnish to your liking.
  6. Spread some mayonaise on the other slice of bread.
  7. Place the 2nd piece of bread atop the sandwhich.
  8. Slice sandwhich diagonally, and enjoy - perhaps with some chips and a cola.
